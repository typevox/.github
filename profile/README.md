## ⚡ What is Typevox?

**Typevox** is a powerful application that turns your voice into instant text across any application. Powered by cutting-edge AI models, it runs entirely on your local machine. No cloud, no subscriptions, and zero data collection.

Whether you're coding, writing emails, or drafting your next novel, Typevox bridges the gap between thought and digital ink.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **🔒 Privacy First:** Your voice recordings are processed in memory and never hit the cloud.
* **🚀 Global Dictation:** Works everywhere. Word, Slack, VS Code, or your browser—if you can type there, you can speak there.
* **🎙️ Offline Intelligence:** Uses local AI models. Transcribe even when you’re off the grid.
* **⚡ Zero Friction:** Hold a hotkey, speak, release. Watch your words appear instantly.
* **🛠️ Pro Tools:** * **GPU Acceleration:** Support for NVIDIA CUDA for lightning-fast processing.
    * **Transcription History:** Searchable local database of everything you've said.
    * **Voice Commands:** Say "new line" or "delete that" to control your workflow hands-free.
